Astronauts Behnken and Hurley name the Crew Dragon spacecraft, Endeavour. It is the third US spacecraft to use that name after 1971's Apollo 15 Command and Service Module and 1992's Space Shuttle orbiter. SECOND STAGE

One Merlin 1D vacuum engine uses RP-1 and LOx. The M-1 DVac engine is a modified M-1D engine with a larger nozzle. It has a vacuum thrust of 210,000 Ibf/95,254 kgf and an Isp of 348 seconds by using a 165:1 area ratio nozzle extension and can throttle down to 39% with its pintle injector chamber design. The pintle was first used in the Apollo Lunar Module Descent Engine (LMDE) for deep throttling and landing on the lunar surface.
